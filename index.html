      Frequently Asked Questions

      Everything you need to know about our rental service

      What do I need to rent a bike?

      You'll need a valid ID, and to be at least 18 years old. For electric bikes, you must be 21 or older.

      What is your cancellation policy?

      You can cancel your reservation free of charge up to 24 hours before your rental period begins. Cancellations within 24 hours will incur a 50% charge.

      What happens if the bike gets damaged?

      All rentals include basic damage protection. You'll be responsible for the first $50 of any damage. For major damage or theft, additional charges may apply..

      Can I extend my rental period?

      Absolutely! Just give us a call at least 2 hours before your rental period ends. Extensions are subject to bike availability.

      Terms & Policies

      Important information about our services

      Terms of Service

      1. Rental Agreement

      By renting a bicycle from RentHub, you agree to our terms and conditions. The rental period begins at the agreed time and ends when the bicycle is returned to our possession.

      2. Rental Period and Fees

      Rental fees are calculated based on the time the bicycle is checked out until it is returned. Overtime fees will be charged at 1.5 times the standard hourly rate for each hour or portion thereof that the bicycle is kept beyond the agreed return time.

      3. Responsibility for Equipment

      You are responsible for the bicycle during the rental period. You agree to return the bicycle in the same condition as when received, except for normal wear and tear.

      4. Cancellations

      Reservations may be cancelled without penalty up to 24 hours prior to the scheduled rental time. Cancellations within 24 hours will be charged 50% of the rental fee.

      Privacy Policy

      1. Information We Collect

      We collect personal information you provide when making a reservation, including your name, email address, phone number, and payment information. We may also collect usage data about how you interact with our website.

      2. How We Use Your Information

      Your information is used to process reservations, communicate with you about your rental, improve our services, and for marketing purposes (with your consent). We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

      3. Data Security

      We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Payment information is encrypted and processed through secure payment gateways.

      4. Your Rights

      You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also opt out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us or using the unsubscribe link in our emails.

      Safety Guidelines

      1. Helmet Use

      We strongly recommend wearing a helmet at all times while riding. Helmets are available for free at our locations.

      2. Traffic Laws

      Riders must obey all local traffic laws and regulations. This includes stopping at stop signs and red lights, riding with traffic, and using hand signals for turns.

      3. Prohibited Activities

      Do not ride under the influence of alcohol or drugs. Never carry passengers unless the bicycle is specifically designed for multiple riders. Avoid using mobile devices while riding.

      4. Maintenance Checks

      Before each ride, check that tires are properly inflated, brakes are working, and all components are secure. If you notice any issues, contact us immediately.

      5. Weather Conditions

      Be aware of weather conditions. Avoid riding in heavy rain, strong winds, or other hazardous conditions that may affect your safety.
